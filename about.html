My Story

My name is Dominick Costanzo, I graduated from SUNY Oneonta in upstate New York with a BA in Graphic Design in 2016. I have been a graphic designer for the past 6 years working at many companies creating digital marketing for websites and social media.

Being a designer, I also interests in the fine arts such as painting, drawing and photography. Not related to this design, I am also a fitness junkie and I compete in long distance running. I’ve been running competitively since the age of 12 and I have developed an obssesion that I can’t give up.

Due to my running from such a young age I have developed a very good work ethic. I am the type of person that is always looking to push the edge of what Im capable of so I can always improve and learn.
